    • About Me

    Became part of the U.S. Marine Corps in 2003. Upon being medically retired for stage III colon cancer, I began to research cancer on my own. I realized that there was a strong relationship with several types of cancers and food. Once I finished chemotherapy, I moved back home to pursue more knowledge in nutrition. I graduated from the University of Arizona with a B.S. in Nutritional Sciences and a minor in chemistry. I had worked several years teaching classes and providing coaching for people who wanted to make changes for the betterment of their health. I am attending a completely different kind of bootcamp, a 12 week course that covers the full-stack curriculum. Please check back as I plan to update this page with realized projects.
